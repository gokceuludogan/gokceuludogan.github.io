Biography

I am a Teaching Assistant in Computer Engineering at Boğaziçi University. Also, I am studying for a PhD degree and hold BSc and MSc degrees in the same department. My research interests include deep learning, cheminformatics and natural language processing.

I am working under the supervision of Arzucan Özgür and Elif Özkırımlı and a member of AILAB and TABILAB.
